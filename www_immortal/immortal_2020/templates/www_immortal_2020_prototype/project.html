China Place

Commercial, hospitality, retail

The "China Place" name was coined with the aim of revitalising precint.

The design premise for the brand identity was derived from a Chinese lattice window design. It captures the idea of being “a window of timeless vibrancy” and references the area’s history.

An environmental graphics system was developed, consisting of building markers, directional and circulation signs.

Local artists were commissioned to create murals that depict the area’s heritage while injecting visual interest and humour.
